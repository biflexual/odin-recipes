Peanut Butter Chocolate Ice Cream

So you wanna make some chocolate peanut butter ice cream? Well let me tell you, this recipe will have you addicted. It is packed with protein and still very tasty. It has the perfect macros to bulk on. For this recipe though, you will need an appliance called a ninja creami.

Here is a list of the ingredients:
  • Peanut butter powder
  • Fairlife Chocolate Milk
  • Sweetener of your choice
  • Stablizer, such as pudding mix or Xantham Gum

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together
  2. Add in the wet ingredients
  3. Mix together and put them into the pints
  4. Put the pints in the freezer.
  5. Finally, take the pints out a day later and mix them in the ninja creami on the light ice cream setting.

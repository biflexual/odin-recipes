Simple Cookies

So you are new to baking and want a simple recipe. Well this is the perfect way to start. It only uses three simple ingredients.

Here are the ingredients:

  • An Egg
  • A Sweetener
  • Peanut butter

Here are the instructions:

  1. Mix the ingredients together until it forms a dough like substance.
  2. Spread them on a sheet inches a part in little balls
  3. Throw them into the oven and cook as desired

Yummy Steak

Ever wanted to experiment with your steaks? Well this is the recipe for you. It is beginner-friendly and it will be sure to taste amazing. However, this recipe may take time, but as long as you prepare it in advance, it will not take much effort. I hope you enjoy!

This recipe involves a marinade, so if that is not for you, try another one of the recipes on this website.

Here is a list of the ingredients:
  • Slap ya mama
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Steak cut of your choice
  • Oil of your choice (optional)

Steps:
  1. Throw your steak in a ziplock bag and mix the ingredients in
  2. Set the bag in the fridge for 3-24 hours depending on the cut of steak, the more tender the steak the less time is needed to marinate.
  3. Take your steak out at least 30 mins before cooking, this ensures that the steak will cook more evenly.
  4. Finally, put the steak on the grill or stovetop and cook as desired
